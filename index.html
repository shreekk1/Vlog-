Chapter One

Where It All Begins

Every story has a beginning, and mine starts in Nepal — a land of breathtaking mountains, ancient festivals, and a warmth that never leaves you no matter how far you roam. Home is not just a place; it's the feeling of colour on your skin, laughter in the grass, and a sky that feels like it belongs only to you.

"Holi is more than a festival of colours — it is the feeling of pure, uninhibited joy. To lie in the grass of Nepal, covered in blue and green and pink, smiling at the sky… that is what it means to be free. That joy travels with me everywhere I go."

Chapter Four

Adventures in the Wild

Between the city's roar, there are trails to hike, fields to wander, and sunsets that stop your breath mid-sentence. Nature is where you find yourself again.

🦅 Hudson Highlands, NY

The trailhead at Hudson Highlands State Park Preserve greeted me with a giant eagle and the promise of forest trails. With a peace sign and a big smile, this hike felt like reclaiming something wild — the need to move, breathe, and exist under open sky.

🌻 Sunflower Fields

A white dress, sandals, and a sea of sunflowers stretching to the horizon. There's something about standing in a sunflower field — they all face the light, and somehow, standing there, so do you.

🌸 Cherry Blossom Season

Spring arrives in pink. There's magic in standing under a full cherry blossom tree — petals drifting down like slow snow, everything soft, fleeting, and breathtaking.

🌊 Lakeside Afternoons

Lake George on a summer afternoon — clear blue water, mountain silhouettes, and nowhere else to be. These are the moments that turn ordinary days into something worth remembering.
